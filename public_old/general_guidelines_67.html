GENERAL GUIDELINES:

  1. All bookings for Air & Train travel will be done for AP employees through Admin at Katreinapadu Office and for others through Admin at HO.
  2. All travel request to be raised on online through ERP travel application. HOD must approve the same.
  3. If the travel is on Sunday/Holiday, employee must raise the request for booking tickets in advance to avoid last minute rush.
  4. Air Travel must be approved by the Managing Partner for non-eligible category.
  5. All Travel expenses should be submitted within one week of travel.
  6. In places where Uber/OLA like facilities are available employees must use that and provide appropriate bills.
  7. The expenses will be reimbursed as per Travel policy. In case of claims having expenses above the limit, only the eligible amount will be reimbursed.
  8. Claims without bills will not be accepted/reimbursed.
  9.  It is encouraged to plan your travel well in advance.
