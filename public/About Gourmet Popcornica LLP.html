ABOUT GOURMET POPCORNICA


As with all things, our journey too started with a single wish - to bring to the cinemas of India, the tastiest popcorn in the world. And what better way to do this than to team up with the world's favourite popcorn supplier, Preferred Popcorn, USA!

In 2014, our partnership with the renowned farmer grown company in Nebraska, USA, allowed us to import kernels to produce the most delicious popcorn premix for movie-goers across the country from our premix factory at Ambattur, Chennai.

In 2018, with the guidance of our American partners, we began using the 75 years old tradition of nurturing corn, to grow the highest quality corn in our very own home state of Andhra Pradesh. From there it was no turning back. Today we grown corn in around 20,000 acres in the Krishna and Godavari basin of Andra Pradesh. And further expanding to Karnataka, Chattisgarh, Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Gujarat. We have our R&D facility at Gurgaon.

With corporate headquarters at Chennai, we have regional sales office at Delhi, Mumbai and Hyderabad.
