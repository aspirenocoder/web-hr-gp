COST TO THE COMPANY

The term Cost to Company (CTC) may in general comprise the following elements, as may be applicable based on each of the employee’s contractual conditions.

  • Basic pay
  • Dearness Allowance
  • House Rent allowance
  • Special allowance
  • Conveyances and medical allowances
  • Company’s contribution to Provident Fund
  • Company’s contribution to Employees’ State Insurance Scheme
  • Variable pay
  • Bonus
  • Such other allowances/reimbursements as may be specified by the Management from time to time

The Basic Pay and all other allowances and benefits shall be administered through a proper Salary structure which is subject to change from time to time to keep pace with market, labour laws and other rules and regulations governing the employment
