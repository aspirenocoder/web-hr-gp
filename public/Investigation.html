Investigation

  1. The Investigation Committee will conduct a fair and unbiased investigation, ensuring the rights of all parties involved.
  2. The complainant, witnesses, and the accused will be interviewed separately, and relevant evidence will be collected.
  3. The investigation process will be completed within a reasonable timeframe ranging from 30 days to 90 days while ensuring the thoroughness of the investigation.
  4. The Investigation Committee will analyse the evidence gathered and determine if sexual harassment has occurred based on the preponderance of evidence standard.
  5. If sexual harassment is established, the Investigation Committee will recommend appropriate disciplinary actions in line with the company's policies and applicable laws.
  6. The recommendations may include warnings, suspension, termination, or any other appropriate measures to address the situation and prevent future incidents.
