TERMS & CONDITIONS:

  1. All employees in field staff role (permanent employees) will get an allowance as per Annexure 7.1 for using their own bike for official field visits. The allowance paid includes cost for servicing the two-wheeler also.
  2. Employees claiming allowance and petrol expenses under this policy will not be eligible to claim fuel expenses as per Annexure 6.2 mentioned for other category of employees.
  3. If there is a variance of 5 % in petrol price, the effect will be given in the fuel reimbursement policy.
  4. Vehicle should be in the name of the employee using it with proper registration, Insurance and PUC (Valid Documents). Employee should hold Driving License in force & always wear a helmet while riding.
  5. An employee must own a bike as per the tenure mentioned in Annexure 7.1. After completion of the tenure, allowance will be stopped until the new bike is bought by an employee.
  6. Logbook / digital record details should be verified by supervisor before approving the fuel bills for the claim every month.
  7. While an employee is claiming this allowance and he is transferred to other dept where he is not eligible for this allowance, he will continue to get the allowance as per tenure mentioned in this vehicle allowance policy.
  8. All the employees who are currently getting vehicle allowance of Rs 2500/- will continue to get the same for the 4th year also as per Annexure 7.1
  9. All the employees who are currently not getting vehicle allowance of Rs 2500/- for reasons such as the vehicle tenure is more than 3 yrs or vehicle tenure is about to complete 3 years, they will get the same now till the completion of 4th year from the date of purchase of vehicle.
  10. Eligible Field staffs from grade 9 to 4, will be allowed to use four-wheeler for official purpose and claim fuel reimbursement at actuals, subject to prior approval.
  11. Fuel reimbursement for Seasonal contract employees in field staff role during seasons will be given at Rs 2.5/KM. Vehicle Maintenance allowance will not be provided to them.
