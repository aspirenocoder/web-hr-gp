ELIGIBILITY FOR OIL PALM FIELD EMPLOYEES OF SARVE SAI

  1. Sarve Sai field employees will get a fixed fuel allowance of Rs 3000/- per month for official field visits. This is inclusive of fuel expenses.
  2. Field employees those who are not getting this allowance, can claim fuel reimbursement at Rs 2.2/KM, subject to prior approval of management.
  3. The provision of using company car will be subject to prior approval of management.
