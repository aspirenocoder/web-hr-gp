DEFINTIONS

  • Performance Appraisal- The process of reviewing the performance of an employee during the appraisal period is called performance appraisal.
  • PMS- Performance management System
  • Appraisee- The person whose performance gets evaluated is an appraisee
  • Appraiser- The person who monitors the job responsibilities/activities of the appraisee regularly is an appraiser.
  • Reviewer- The person who reviews the evaluation done by appraiser for an appraisee.
  • Self-Appraisal-The self-appraisal is intended to facilitate the appraisee to put forth his achievements as perceived by him/her, against the targets set. This will facilitate the appraiser to be well-informed about the appraisee’s achievements and that the appraisee gets a chance to express his views and also provide the appraiser comprehensive feedback about the needs and expectations of the subordinate.
  • KRA: Key Result Areas; KPI – Key Performance Indicators
  • Review Period – 1st June – 31st May
