ESCALATION PROCESS:

In order to ensure that the employees have a fair say in the complete process, the performance management system has incorporated an escalation protocol in it.

  1. The process remains incomplete till the employee accepts the rating arrived at.
  2. If an employee is not satisfied with the rating arrived at during the one-to-one discussion, the employee is advised to escalate the concern to the reviewer (HOD).
  3. The rating given by the reviewer in the escalation process is final and the rating is informed to the management and the appraisal process is documented.
