PURPOSE:

Gourmet Popcornica believes in building a culture of performance that would support continuous improvement of every employee’s performance, thereby contributing to the organization’s continued growth. This policy explains the mechanism of translating this belief into practice through the process of performance management which ensures fairness and transparency in:

  • Setting objectives and evaluating performance
  • Providing performance-related and developmental feedback to the employee
  • Recognizing and rewarding employees based on their performance
  • Improving effectiveness and productivity of employees

SCOPE : This policy is applicable to all confirmed employees of Gourmet Popcornica and Sarve Sai.
