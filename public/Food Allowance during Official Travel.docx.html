FOOD ALLOWANCE DURING OFFICIAL TRAVEL

This policy also helps employees manage their food expenses during their travel within India, which includes accommodation or lodging, food allowances, local conveyance, travel, settlement and reimbursements. Refer Annexure 6.3

  1. Claims must be supported with bills as far possible. In cases where one could not get the bill, employee should submit it with special approval of Department head / Business head.
  2. Employees are encouraged to select accommodation with complimentary breakfast facilities.
