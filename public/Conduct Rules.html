CONDUCT RULES

[DO’S] – EVERY EMPLOYEE SHALL 

  1. Maintain absolute integrity at all times.
  2. Maintain absolute devotion to duty at all times.
  3. Maintain independence and impartiality in the discharge of duties.
  4. Maintain a responsible and decent standard of conduct in private life.
  5. Render prompt and courteous service to the public.
  6. Observe proper decorum on and off the workplace.
  7. Promptly report to superiors civil/criminal procedures against him/her, if any, in a Court of Law and the circumstances connected therewith.
  8. Keep away from demonstrations organised by political parties.
  9. Maintain political neutrality in their official dealings involving the company.
  10. Avoid habitual indebtedness or insolvency.
  11. Act in accordance with Management policies
  12. Maintain courtesy while dealing with the public
  13. Limit the use of mobile phones while in the premises apart from official work.

[DON’TS] – NO EMPLOYEE SHALL 

  1. Indulge in acts detrimental to the interest of Gourmet Popcornica and Sarve Sai.
  2. Adopt dilatory tactics in his/her dealings with the public.
  3. Practice untouchability.
  4. Associate himself/herself with any banned organisations.
  5. Join any association or demonstration whose objects or activities are prejudicial to the interest of the sovereignty and integrity of India, public order or morality.
  6. Accept lavish or frequent hospitality from any individual, industrial or commercial firms, organisations, etc., having official dealings with him/her.
  7. Lend money to or borrow money from or deposit money as a member or agent, with any person, firm or private company with whom he/she is likely to have official dealings.
  8. Approach subordinates for standing surety for loans taken from private sources either by his/her, relations, or friends.
  9. Appear in a public place in a state of intoxication. Indulge in any act of sexual harassment of any kind at workplace.
  10. Enter any private correspondence with Foreign Embassies or Missions/High Commissions.
  11. Wilfully involve in insubordination or disobedience, whether alone or in combination with others to any lawful and reasonable order of a supervisor.
  12. Involve in act of theft or dishonesty in connection with the Managements’ activities or property.
  13. Wilfully damage to or loss of the company’s goods or property.
  14. Take or give bribes or any illegal gratification.
  15. Habitually come late.
  16. Habitually breach any law applicable at the Management.
  17. Habitually neglect of work.
  18. Frequently repeat any misconduct or omission.
  19. Involve in strike at work or inciting others to strike work.
  20. Contravene the provisions of any law, or rule having the force of law.
  21. Act in a manner prejudicial to the interests of Gourmet Popcornica.
  22. Involve in the act of drunkenness or riotous or disorderly or indecent behaviour in the premises or outside premises where such behaviour is related to or connected with the employment.
  23. Involve in gambling within the premises of the office or other place of work, where it is prohibited.
  24. Smoke within the premises of the Office or other place of work, where it is prohibited.
  25. Collect without the permission of the competent authority, of any money (within the premises of the Management) except as sanctioned by any law of the land for the time being in force or rules of the Management.
  26. Sleep while on duty.
  27. Watch movies, play video games, Browse/Create social media content (Instagram, Facebook, Snapchat, Youtube, Twitter, Share trading, etc) it is strictly prohibited.
  28. Commission any act which amounts to a criminal offence involving moral turpitude.
  29. Absent from the employee’s appointed place of work without permission or sufficient cause.
  30. Commission any act subversive of discipline or good behaviour.
  31. Wilfully prolong absence from duty without reasonable cause.
  32. Fail to maintain a responsible and decent standard of conduct in private life, thereby bringing discredit to the company.
  33. Have obtained an advance from the Management for a specific purpose such as building a house, purchase of conveyance, leave travel or for any other declared purpose, but not utilising the advance for the intended purpose within the period specified and diverting the money for any other use.
  34. In any act unbecoming of an employee of the Management.
  35. Assault or abuse or insult any of the officers or other employees within the premises of office or other place of work or outside.
  36. Interfere in the work of other employees.
  37. Approach higher authorities directly or through other persons for promotion or any other personal favour or gain except through proper channels.
  38. Write anonymous or pseudonymous letters criticising the Management or any other Manager/ employee of Gourmet Popcornica.
  39. Spread false rumours or giving false information or making defamatory statements (written or oral) which tend to bring the Management or its Managers into disrepute.
  40. Carry money-lending or any other private business.
  41. Wilfully fail to appear before the Medical Board, when called upon to do so.
  42. Involve in abetment or attempt at abetment act which amounts to misconduct.
