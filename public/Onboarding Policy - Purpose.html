PURPOSE

Employee onboarding is the process in which new hires get familiar with the organization, the people, and the culture of the company they've just joined. It's a key stage of the employee life cycle.

It is proposed to give a joining kit containing the following items to all new hire with the maximum value of one joining kit not exceeding Rs 500/-

*Any new hire re-joining us within 2 years of his exit is not eligible for this kit.
