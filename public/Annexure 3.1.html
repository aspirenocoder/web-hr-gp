Annexure 3.1 - New Employee Joining Checklist

S.no

Name of the form

Content

1

Employment Application Form

  1. Personal information (full name, date of birth, contact details).
  2. Job position, department, and reporting manager.
  3. Previous employment history, service certificates
  4. Educational qualifications.
  5. Emergency contact information.
  6. Reference details

2

ID Card Request Form

  1. Attach two stamp-sized photographs (with the name written on the back).
  2. Requested details for ID card issuance (name, department, start date).

3

Statutory Documents

  1. PF Form 2 and 11
  2. Gratuity Declaration form
  3. ESIC declaration form

4

Identity Proof documents

  1. Government issued photo ID (e.g., Aadhar card, passport).
  2. PAN Card
  3. Address Proof

5

Bank Details Form (if provided on the first day)

  1. Bank account information for salary deposit.
  2. IFSC code, account number, and branch details

6

Tax Declaration Forms

  1. Previous employment income and tax details (if applicable).
  2. Declaration of investments for tax saving (if applicable).

7

Health Insurance Enrolment Form (if applicable)

  1. Personal details and medical history
  2. Medical nomination form - Details of dependents to be covered under the health insurance plan

8

Confidentiality Agreement or Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

Reading and signing the agreement that outlines the confidentiality terms regarding company information.

9

Workplace Safety and Sexual Harassment Policy Acknowledgment Form

Acknowledgment of understanding and agreeing to comply with workplace safety and anti-sexual harassment policies.
