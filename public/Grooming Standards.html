GROOMING STANDARDS:

Employees must wear company given uniform from Monday to Thursday. They can wear company provided T-shirts on Friday and Saturday. Office employees can wear formal colour dress / business casuals on Saturdays.

  1. Attire: Employees should dress in clean and appropriate clothing that is consistent with their job roles. Avoid overly revealing or offensive attire and ensure that clothing is in good repair.
  2. Hairstyling and Beard for Men: All employees should be well-groomed. Hairstyle must be groomed for both male & female employees. Male employees must groom their facial hair (eg. moustaches, beards), clean shave is encouraged.
  3. Jewellery and Accessories: Employees should wear minimal and non-disruptive jewellery and accessories. Avoid wearing items that may be considered distracting or unsafe for the job.
  4. Tattoos and Piercings: Tattoos and piercings should be modest and non-offensive. Any visible tattoos should not promote discrimination, violence, or offensive content, and facial piercings should be kept to a minimum or removed during work hours.
  5. Fragrances: Employees are discouraged from wearing powerful perfumes, colognes, or attars that may cause discomfort or allergies to coworkers. A subtle and considerate use of deodorant to avoid stench if any is only recommended.
  6. Floral Hair Accessories: Female employees should avoid wearing strong-smelling flowers in their hair that may be disruptive to others. Subtle and non-fragrant floral accessories are acceptable if they do not cause discomfort to colleagues.
