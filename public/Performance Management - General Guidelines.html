GENERAL GUIDELINES

  1. Every year, individual employees must prepare their Goal sheets in alignment with their department/division goals and submit to HR  

  1. The assessment is done against the mutually agreed Key Result Areas (KRAs) & the associated Goals/KPI’ s as per the Goal sheet, set at the beginning of the year, between the Appraisee and the Appraiser.

  1. The Goals agreed upon mutually between the appraisee & the reporting authority, are to be reviewed mid-way at the end of 1 year period.

  1. The performance pay given to an individual will be decided every year based on company performance (20%) and individual performance (80%).

  1. The Increments and variable pay will be given based on the rating given and percentage decided by the management every year during appraisal. The performance rating scale is given in Annexure 8.1

  1. As per the current practice, the increment will be made effective from 1st June every year. However, this may change as per Management decision from time to time.

  1. As per the current practice, the Promotion and Variable Pay will be made effective from 1st July every year. However, this may change as per Management decision from time to time.
